Why Phakdee Biz (Because nobody else will help poor people to the max)

We at phakdee biz have created this platform for you to reach out to us since the pandemic which has disrupted the global business. During Covid-19, multiple business has either closed down or gone bankrupt, which has left many hard-working skilled people without jobs.

So, we decide to create a platform similar to what they have already in western countries, but this is for Thailand only at the moment. Phakdee Biz is supported by multiple high net-worth induvials from international countries. Our partners and investors have put aside one million dollars (thirty-five million THB) for our vision to help underprivileged, uneducated and unfortunate people right across Thailand

Our Service
